How to Play Tide Twist

You control a glowing sea pearl drifting through a narrow underwater channel.

  • Tap / Space / Enter — Flip your fin to counter the current
  • The tide alternates between Left and Right current
  • Pass through the gap in each Reef Gate to score
  • Perfect Pass (near center of gap) earns bonus points
  • Avoid hitting the walls or the reef bars
  • Speed, gap size, and flip frequency all increase over time!
